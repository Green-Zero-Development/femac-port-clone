The Story of FEMAC Security Solutions

When we started out in 1990 to provide banks and credit unions with electronic and physical security solutions and service we soon realized there were businesses and home owners that needed the same level of care that we could provide. We believed we could build long lasting relationships built on the willingness to listen, mutual respect for our clients and co-workers, and trust. We don't just want satisfied customers and employees, we want happy ones. It's just that simple.

We believe in doing quality installations, and providing fast, reliable, and courteous service. We use only the best products and we will not put them in the field until we have used and tested them ourselves.

We are passionate about what we do. Providing Smart, Simple Security Solutions is our way. Let us Earn your Confidence and we will Strive to Maintain it everyday.
