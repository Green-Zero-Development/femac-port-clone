Geofencing

Another feature of the Virtual Keypad App is "Geofencing". It allows you to create Virtual Fences using the GPS on your phone. The Alarm System recognizes when the fence has been exited or entered triggering a Favorite to activate. For example, a Favorite named "Home" could be activated when the you enter the Geofence around your residence, triggering system features to adjust the thermostat, turn on the lights, and open the garage door as you turn the corner of your street. You could also create a "Not Home" Favorite, triggered when you exit the Geofence, to set back your thermostat, turn off the lights, close the garage door, and lock the entry doors. With up to 20 Geofences you can program it to give you Alerts when the kids get home from school and so much more!
